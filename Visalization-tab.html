Latitude vs Humidity

Majority of the humidity data is uniformed but latitudes located closer to the Equator have greater percentage of humidity. Higher recordings of humidity can be found in the northern hemisphere than in the southern hemisphere. This is a result of greater number of tropical climate regions throughout the northern latitudes than in the southern latitudes.

Latitude vs Temperature

Latitudes closer to the Equator, zero degrees, have warmer temperarure recordings. The farther away the latitude degree is from the Equator, the cooler maximum temperature. This latitutde relationship with temperature correlates with our knowledge of cities located farther in the northern and southern hemispheres are closer to the poles and have cooler weather.
